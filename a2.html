ANNABELLE

Director: David F. Sandberg.

Film series: ANNABELLE:CREATION

Actors: Stephanie Sigman, Talitha Eliana Bateman, Lulu Wilson, Philippa Coulthard, Grace Fulton, Samara Lee.

Imdb: 6.5

This is second installment in the ANNABELLE universe. To begin with, this movie is wayyyy better than the first with a ton of jump scares and a good plot. The way they connected the 1st and 2nd movie was remarkable and helps us understand the first movie. Talitha Bateman did a wonderful job. It's definitely a worthy entry -- if not one of the best -- in the Conjuring universe of horror... Annabelle: Creation should be seen late at night, in the dark.
